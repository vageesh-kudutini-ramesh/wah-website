Wah! Story?

Hi, Im Prasad Chundi, Founder & CEO of Wah!.
As a homeowner, it's always stressful to get hold of vendors and negotiate prices on home repairs or improvements.


Wah! started with a simple idea just like Costco negotiates bulk discounts for its members, Wah! offers pre-negotiated (30-70%) bulk discounted rates for all homeowners in that community.


Whether its a maintenance tune-up for Garage, Plumbing, Appliances, Electrical, or seasonal Christmas lighting, pest control, or even unique situations that require attorneys or CPAs Wah! has got it for you.
